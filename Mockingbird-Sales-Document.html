Executive Summary

Mockingbird is a production-ready social media automation platform that enables content creators, social media managers, and agencies to publish content simultaneously across Facebook, Twitter/X, Instagram, and Telegram from a single dashboard.

Built with modern technologies including Next.js 15, React 19, and PostgreSQL, the platform features AI-powered content transformation with support for multiple LLM providers (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, Mistral, Ollama), comprehensive analytics, and real-time publishing feedback.

The platform addresses a growing $22B+ social media management market with unique differentiators including multi-AI provider support, Instagram Stories integration, and per-account content transformation rules.

AI-Powered Intelligence

Multi-Provider Flexibility: Unlike competitors locked to a single AI provider, Mockingbird supports OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, Mistral AI, and even local Ollama instances - giving users control over cost, quality, and data privacy.

Smart Transformation: Each connected account can have custom AI transformation rules. A fitness brand's Instagram can use different tone and hashtags than their professional LinkedIn content - all automated.

Test Before Publish: Built-in prompt testing ensures content quality before going live. Users can preview transformations and iterate on prompts without risking live posts.
